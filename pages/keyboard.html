Why Test Your Keyboard?

A functional keyboard is essential for efficient and comfortable typing. Our Keyboard Tester empowers you to proactively identify and address any issues, ensuring that each keystroke is smooth and responsive.

Don't let keyboard issues slow you down. Try our Keyboard Tester now and ensure a seamless typing experience.

How to Use:

  1. Open the Keyboard Tester in your preferred browser.
  2. To test numeric keypad be make sure Num Lock is ON.
  3. Press the START button provided below.
  4. Press, All the keys, one by one.
  5. check both of ENTER, SHIFT, and CTRL keys, separately.
  6. The detected keys will be highlighted, and a key press sound will be played.
  7. Press Reset button to Test the keyboard again.
